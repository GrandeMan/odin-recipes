Pizza Sauce

Description

The key to a good sauce is finding good canned tomatoes. Essentially, the tomatoes are 90% of the recipe. You only use the other ingredients to enchance the flavour of the tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • 2 28-oz. cans Bianco DiNapoli whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
